WHY CHOOSING US

Centralized Monitoring or (CMS)

CMS is a proactive concept that takes control of the Security operation at the client site. Moreover, CMS will ensure the relationship between manpower and electronic security systems is followed and monitored effectively. The CMS officer at site will act as the main controlling force for the manpower patrolling personnel, and maintain an overall view of the siteâ€™s activities via CCTV camera systems

Guard Patrols

The placement of our security guards is what generally places Kay Jay Security in the position to confidently describe the implementation of our security guard project as authentic. Our security guards are trained to make visible foot patrols throughout their posts in undetermined patterns. We encourage our personnel to interact with your patrons and employees in addition to standing a fixed post. The use of Guard Tour Patrolling Systems will aid this process. The Guard patrols are further enhanced and monitored by the CMS officers monitoring from the CMS control room. With the use of two-way radios, the CMS officers will guide the patrolling officers with the guidence of a CCTV surveillence system at site.

Electronics security

Kay Jay Security believes in combining Manpower and Electronic Security systems in order to deliver high quality and effective security solutions to client sites. The marriage of these entities will ensure increased productiveness in the job roles of our security personnel, and moreover thrive to deliver full proof, proactive security solutions.

Kay Jay Security supervisory personnel

Supervisors are required to possess a minimum of 7 years of prior experience in the same filed, or encompass law enforcement experience. Our supervisors are charged with scheduling, assigning security posts, and acting as liaison between the Board of Directors and our client. Supervisory personnel are available to you 24 hours every day in order maintain quality assurance and customer service on behalf of Kay Jay Security. Unlike most other security establishments, Kay Jay Security employees an area Manager and a divisional officer to supervise each Client site.

Twenty Four Hour Customer Service

Our offices are staffed around the clock with customer service representatives for your convenience. While most of our competitors work from their homes, Kay Jay Security staffs fully functional offices. Our customer service representatives are not salespeople; they are security professionals assigned to light duty or administrative functions. Desk personnel are utilized as watch-commanders with full authority to make supervisory decisions for Kay Jay Security.
