WHO WE ARE

Today’s threats to companies are increasingly complex and intense at a time when business operations are becoming more interrelated and global. Investment in Security can help significantly reduce a company’s vulnerability to the many factors that threaten performance. Kay Jay Security is a private Limited Company that has been in business for well over 40 years. Over the years Kay Jay Agencies has protected Billions of Rupees worth of property and valuables in Sri-Lanka.


Kay Jay Security provides a comprehensive range of security services to meet the demand of any business organization or households throughout Sri-Lanka. At present we provide security services to a wide selection of private and public sector establishments. Our clients include many leading banks, blue chip companies, FTZ factories, and hotels. Our diligence and reliability have earned us a reputation as a leader in security, and most importantly we have gained the trust and confidence of our clients. Due to the speedy advancements of global technology, Kay Jay security services diversified into the electronic security industry, thus offering our clients hi-tech electronic security systems such as CCTV camera systems, Access control systems, visitor management systems and the like.

OUR VISION

To be the most preferred provider of security services in Sri Lanka.

OUR MISSION

To achieve the highest standards in our business operations via strategic management decisions, motivating our personnel, and most importantly maintaining a good relationship with our clients.

Core Values


CUSTOMER-DRIVEN

We value quality partnerships, integrity, exceptional customer service, personal growth, and community leadership. We will be respectful, empathetic, responsive, quality focused, caring and consistent in all customer relations. We will always keep the customer at the forefront of our decisions and our actions.


TEAMWORK

Every member of the KayJay Team will contribute their individual time and talent in a coordinated effort to ensure the company will consistently meet its’ strategic goals. We will communicate our Vision, Mission and Values in a clear, consistent and transparent manner so that all team members understand their role and requirements in achieving these goals.


INTEGRITY

In all faces of our business, our actions shall remain consistent. We will gain trust by honoring our commitments, exemplifying honesty and integrity to our customers, our employees and our community.


INNOVATIVE

The products, systems and technologies that we deliver to our customers will represent the leading edge in our industry. We will partner with companies that are forward looking and that provide us with the highest quality custom security solutions available.


ACCOUNTABILITY

Each member of our team agrees to be accountable for ownership of their actions in relation to other members of the team, our various partners and especially to our customers.
